The future of static site generation, powered by AI.

Transform your content into a high-quality, deployable website with JennGen.

JennGen is an advanced static site generator that leverages the power of AI to create stunning websites with ease. Whether you're a developer looking to streamline your workflow or a business owner in need of a professional web presence, JennGen has got you covered.

Getting Started

  1. Get your OpenAI key from https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
  2. Use npx to get JennGen and render the current folder: npx jenngen .
  3. Open http://localhost:3000 to view it in the browser.

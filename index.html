Getting Started

  1. Get your OpenAI key from https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
  2. Use npx to get JennGen and render the current folder: npx jenngen .
  3. Open http://localhost:3000 to view it in the browser.

JennGen simplifies the process of creating static sites by leveraging the power of AI. It's perfect for developers who value efficiency and quality in their web projects.

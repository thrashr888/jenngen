Introducing JennGen

JennGen is an AI static site generator that helps you create beautiful and responsive websites with ease. With JennGen, you can focus on your content while we take care of the code.

Getting Started

  1. Get your OpenAI key from https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
  2. Use npx to get jenngen and render the current folder: npx jenngen .
  3. Open http://localhost:3000 to view it in the browser.
